About The Church

MFM places a strong focus on youth and teenage ministries, providing spiritual guidance and activities tailored to younger members. The teenage church aims to engage youths through various programs that teach them about faith, encourage community involvement, and equip them with spiritual tools to navigate life’s challenges. These programs often include Bible study sessions, workshops, retreats, and social activities that foster a sense of belonging and encourage moral values.

The MFM Teenage Church is characterized by its dynamic approach to worship and community outreach. Events often feature music, drama, and other creative expressions of faith. The church also runs evangelistic initiatives aimed at outreach among young people, addressing issues such as addiction, societal pressures, and moral dilemmas through targeted support and counseling.
